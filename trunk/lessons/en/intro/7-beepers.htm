7. Beepers

In this lesson, we will learn about the all important beepers!

Beep! Beep!

Reeborg's world includes beepers. These are small objects (drawn fairly big on the screen) that make a small beeping sound when they are turned on. Reeborg can only hear beepers if he is standing right on top of them, on the same corner. He can pick them up and carry them (turned off) in his pockets, or he can put them down (turning them on automatically).

You can instruct Reeborg to pick up beepers, through the pick_beeper() command, or to put some down, through the put_beeper() command. If you ask Reeborg to pick up beepers where there are none, or to put down beepers if he doesn't carry any, he will complain and shut down.

Changing beepers

You can easily change Reeborg's world by adding beepers. To do so, you just "right-click" on any intersection and select the number of beepers from 0 (used to remove any beepers present), 1, 2, 3, ..., 14, 15, 20, 40, 60, 80 or 99.
